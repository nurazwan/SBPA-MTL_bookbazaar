A one-stop learning hub for the publishing industry. This publishing course is created for students to learn the ins and outs of book publishing, which includes editing, design, production, distribution and book retailing, marketing, and more.

Our annual publishing course has a practical component, and you can enrol in an internship programme with us. It is a ten-week working experience with our established publishers in Singapore and it comes with a recognised certificate at the end of the course.

Do register your interest for the publishing course or for a place for our internship programme, and we will contact you shortly. The publishing course will be conducted over 10 classes on a weekly basis from mid May till end July.

In conjunction with the MTL Book Bazaar, we are pleased to offer a preview of the course. The introduction course for 'The World of Publishing' is free for your viewing. Other available courses require registration, so please click on the course to express your interest.
